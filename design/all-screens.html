Complete Navigation Flow

GET / → Market View newest market, or idx=0
POST ?action=nav → Market View idx ± 1, wraps around
POST ?action=bet → Place Bet slider to pick amount, side from button
POST ?action=confirm → Confirmation deduct votes, update market totals
POST ?action=create → Create Form input field for question text
POST ?action=submit_market → Market View new market created, shown immediately
POST ?action=resolve → Market View market closed, payouts distributed
